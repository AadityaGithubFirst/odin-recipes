Boozy Banana Bread

Description

Banana Bread is a delicious savoury product that diguises itself as a bread but we all know that it is actually just cake.
Banana Bread is a brilliant ice breaker as well helps bring everyone together for a tasty treat.
Like its name says banana bread is made from mashed banana. Did you know NAtional Banana Bread day is Febraury 23.
there are various variations of banana bread

Ingredients

  • cooking spray
  • 2 cups of all purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 5 very ripe bananas, mashed
  • 3/4 cups of brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup butter, softened
  • 2 eggs
  • 3 drops almond extract
  • 3 drops amaretto (almond flavoured liquer)

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degree F or 175 degree C. Spray a loaf span with cooking spray.
  2. Whisk flour, salt, and baking soda together in a bowl.
  3. Mix bananas, brown sugar, butter, eggs, almond extract, and amaretto together in a
    separate bowl. Add flour mixture; mix just until flour is incorporated, about 1 minute.
    Transfer mixture to the prepared loaf pan.
  4. Bake in the preheated oven until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out
    clean, about 1 hour. Let cool before serving, about 30 minutes.
