Small Batch Peanut Butter Powder M&M Cookies

Description

Cookies are some of the best things that any grandma can make. They crumble at just the right parts
making your mouth just drool in awe seeing as to how can such a delicious treat exist. This is one of the few
foods where I would have to admit that the gooier it is the better it is

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup powdered peanut butter
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 2 1/2 tablespoons unsalted butter, softenend
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon peanut butter flavour
  • 1/4 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/4 cup mini candy-coated milk chocolates

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degree F or 175 degree C. Line a baking sheet with
    parchment paper.
  2. Combine powdered peanut butter, sugar, butter, flour, egg, salt, peanut butter flavor,
    and baking powder in a bowl. Fold in mini M&MsÂ®.
  3. Drop dough by spoonfuls onto the prepared baking sheet.
  4. Bake in the preheated oven until cookies are lightly browned, about 12 minutes.
